# Presentation-Portfolio
Academic presentations and visual research summaries created in PowerPoint, Canva, and Prezi. Focus on structured storytelling, complex topics explanation, and clear visual communication.

This repository contains a small collection of academic presentations created during my university studies. The goal of these projects was to present complex topics in a clear, structured, and visually engaging way.

The presentations were created using **PowerPoint**, **Canva**, and **Prezi** and focus on transforming research and analytical information into understandable visual narratives.

Presentations

- Risk Management

  Presentation explaining the fundamentals of risk management, including risk identification, probability–impact analysis, and mitigation strategies.

- Biodiversity Conservation Strategies

Presentation discussing approaches to biodiversity protection and the environmental challenges related to war and human activity.

- Technology, Market, and Expansion Beyond Borders

Presentation analyzing how technologies enter markets and scale internationally.

- Investment in Human Capital

Presentation exploring the concept of human capital and its role in economic development.
